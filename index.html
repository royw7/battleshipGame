Welcome to Battleship

You will begin by setting up your ships, place them wisely!

Each turn you get up to 5 shots, one shot for each ship you have remaining

Click on a tile to fire a torpedo, if it hits it will be red, otherwise it will be white

Destroy all of your oppenents ship to win!

Likewise if you lose all your ships you lose!


There are 5 ships in this game

Aircraft Carrier: 5 Spaces Long and is Purple

Battleship: 4 Spaces Long and is Blue

Submarine: 5 Spaces Long and is Yellow

Destroyer: 5 Spaces Long and is Green

Patrol Ship: 2 Spaces Long and is Orange
